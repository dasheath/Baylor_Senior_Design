My Senior Design Project

This page contains a design overview for our project.

Objective

Our goal to was design a simple demonstration of fuel injection which conveys the fundamentals to a non-technical audience. Our target audience is non-technical adults and children. We also sought to make the point that a physical disability does not prevent one from pursuing engineering.

Mechanical Design

Piping overview. Fluid mechanics.

Electrical Circuit Design

Our circuit takes in AC power from the wall, converts to DC power to power our Arduino, relay board, and valve and to provide voltage to our button. If we wish for the motor to be powered, we send a signal to a relay to close the contacts connects AC line to the outlet.
