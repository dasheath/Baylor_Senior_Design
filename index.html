My Senior Design Project

This page contains a design overview for our project.

Requirements

The core thrust of Junior Design is to construct an autonomousrobot/mechatronic device. This task requires the collaboration and clever ideas of students with various aptitudes including Solidworks, programming, and circuit design.
The assignment for our semester was to drive your robot down a course and have it drop balls into Y-tubes on the first pass, turn around, then place balls on top of the Y-tubes on the return trip.

Mechanical Design

The robot used a single DC motor to drive using a track setup. We had two identical dispensors which were controlled by stepper motors. Our base was made of wood, our dispensors were 3D printed in ABS, and our tracks were plastic (from VEX).

Model

Electrical and Code

Our task as electrical engineers was to create a system to drive the robot down the course and back, and to design a system to control stepper motors in the dispensors.
We created a DC motor control circuit with direction control.
